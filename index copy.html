About Vinayak Corporation

Vinayak Corporation is a leading importer and supplier of high-quality sanitaryware and plastic bathroom products. We specialize in toilet seat covers, toilet fittings, concealed tanks, and a wide range of innovative, durable sanitary solutions. With a strong commitment to quality and customer satisfaction, Vinayak Corporation delivers products that combine functionality with modern design.
